* Nail Trimming

Dog grooming services include a lot of nail clipping. The nails of a dog grow, and if they are not clipped properly or in a timely manner, they can grow wildly and curl. This causes discomfort to the dogs and may even puncture their paws. Such events can lead to the dogs contracting more unpleasant ailments. A nail clipper is used to trim your nails. Inexperienced users, on the other hand, might inflict harm to the dog when using it. As a result, expert nail cutting is recommended to keep your dog quiet and relaxed.

In Kathmandu, hand stripping is an uncommon but necessary dog grooming service. It entails removing dead hair from the coat to make way for younger, stronger hair to grow in its place.
